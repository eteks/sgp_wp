Who we are

________

Specialty Glass Products (SGP) is an ISO 9001:2008 compliant, custom manufacture of components made of glass, quartz, sapphire and other hard materials. Since 1961 SGP has specialized in the fabrication and CNC machining of precision parts for a wide range of industries including: Biomedical, Optical, aerospace, laser manufacturing, semiconductor, fiber optic and other fields with demanding, tight tolerance applications.
